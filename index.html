Opening

Twenty years of governance. One more layer on top.

Da Blue Box puts together technology, knowledge and experience to deliver Global IT consulting, operations, governance and support. After 20+ years of managed services, compliance, and audit work across South Florida, the next line of operations is an orchestration layer that sits above the existing stack and compresses the work that currently consumes principal hours.

COBIT and ITIL give the governance frame. ICM gives the methodology for the layer on top.

The Eduba frame, applied to DBB

The operational work Da Blue Box already delivers splits into three layers.

60% Traditional code & infrastructure

Microsoft 365 administration, Control4 installs, network monitoring, ticketing, endpoint management.

30% Rule-based logic

Compliance checks, SLA enforcement, change-management workflow, runbook execution.

10% Genuine AI work

Audit-report assembly from heterogeneous sources, anomaly interpretation across monitoring signals, document extraction in compliance engagements.

Building on the 10% without disturbing the 60 or 30 is the orchestration problem Eduba exists to solve.
